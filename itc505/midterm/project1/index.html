Development Process

Developing the "Personality Adventure" involved a systematic approach to building an interactive narrative experience using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Starting with the structure, the HTML was organized to create distinct sections for the game setup and story progression. CSS was applied to enhance the visual elements, ensuring a pleasant and engaging user interface. The JavaScript was used to manage the game flow, implementing a story object with various stages and their respective outcomes.

Challenges Faced

One of the major challenges was structuring the multiple pathways of the story to converge on eight distinct endings. Ensuring each choice led to a coherent and conclusive end required meticulous planning and implementation. Additionally, selecting appropriate images to complement each stage while maintaining a coherent visual narrative posed its own challenge.

Insights and Creative Choices

The color scheme choice, predominantly a calming blue, was made to foster a relaxed and engaging atmosphere, suitable for a personality test. The image selection process aimed to reinforce the narrative and emotional context at each stage, promoting a more immersive experience for the player.

Insights gained included the criticality of engaging visuals, concise yet relatable text, and the importance of flexibility in storytelling to accommodate diverse user choices.
