Leap Year Calculator Addendum

Problem Solved:

The JavaScript code determines whether the provided date of birth corresponds to a leap year.

Overview of Solution:

  • **Input Validation:** Checks if the entered date is valid.
  • **Leap Year Calculation:** Determines if the year of the provided date is a leap year.
  • **Display Result:** Shows a message indicating whether the birth year is a leap year.

Difficulties Encountered:

  • **Ensuring Valid Input:** Validating the input date to handle potential errors like incorrect formats or invalid dates.
  • **Leap Year Logic:** Ensuring the correct leap year calculation using the logical conditions (divisibility by 4, 100, and 400).

Solutions to Difficulties:

  • **Input Validation:** Utilized `isNaN(selectedDate.getTime())` to confirm a valid date entry.
  • **Leap Year Logic:** Employed conditional statements `(year % 4 === 0 && year % 100 !== 0) || (year % 400 === 0)` to accurately determine leap years.
